CHASM OF BELLIGERENCE

The Story So Far...

For causes known only to you and Crom, you have been cast into THE CHASM OF BELLIGERENCE. To escape, you must find the Codex, Crown, and Ring of the Goblin Lord Mukbang, for some reason.

Controls

  • WASD or arrow keys to move (diagonal movement supported)
  • Q to use healing potion
  • Z to change zoom level

Stuff

  • The Sword grants a +1 attack bonus.
  • The Shield grants a +1 defense bonus.
  • Health Potions restore 1 HP; you can carry 1 at a time.
  • Runes grant bonuses to attack, defense or max HP.
  • The Torch improves visibility.

Tips

  • Mo' levels = mo' monsters
  • Level up every 10 points
  • You can travel back and forth between levels
